ASL for: love-it
\n
\n\n\n\n
\n \n\n

Definition

"love it"; a favorite

\n

Synonyms

\n\n

Note

The ASL "word" is used to refer to the emotional effection for another person as in "I love you." But, the ASL word "love-it" is usually referred to an object, an activity, or a person (as in admiration or favorite). E.g. "I love playing soccer."\r\n
